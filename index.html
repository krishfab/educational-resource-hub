Introduction

An education hub is a strategically planned location that concentrates a critical mass of educational, training, knowledge production, and innovation initiatives, often involving both local and international actors. These hubs aim to enhance a country or region's competitiveness by attracting students, developing a skilled workforce, and fostering a knowledge-based economy. Education hubs can be categorized into student hubs, talent hubs, and knowledge-innovation hubs, each with distinct focuses and objectives.

About
